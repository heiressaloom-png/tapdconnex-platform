03

Three axes, never collapsed

The most important mental model. Three separate scores answer three different questions. A relationship can be high value, clearly understood, yet poorly captured — and that last case is exactly what the Pro +5-minute window exists to fix.

StrengthIs this worth my time? — 0–100, gated so warmth alone can't read as a deal.
ConfidenceHow sure are we we read it right? — 0–100, independent of strength, floored when evidence is thin.
CompletenessDid we capture enough to act? — a percentage, split into what you can fill now and what to ask next time.
04

Why you can trust the number

These are deterministic rules, not an eager model's opinion. They're what separate relationship intelligence from a chatbot that calls everything an opportunity.

The Gate

Warmth can't fake a deal

Strength is capped at 49 unless a qualifying signal is present — action intent, urgency, authority, resource, or a strong template signal. A friendly chat with no real intent physically cannot score hot.

The Floor

Thin evidence can't be certain

Confidence is capped at 70 when there are fewer than two pieces of evidence, or all of it is indirect. One vague quote can't produce high confidence.

Provenance

You can't self-fill an opportunity

A claim you supply about the contact in the re-record window adds zero to the score. Only what the contact actually said carries weight.

Hallucination guard

No quote, no signal

Every signal is tied to a verbatim quote present in the transcript. If the quote isn't there, the signal is discarded. "The AI never invents" is mechanical, not a hope.

06

Ten doors into the engine

Each template is a plain-language intent the user picks before recording. Three are free forever; seven are Pro. Each defines the required context to act — and its key field (★) sets the relationship's clock.

07

The rules that never bend

  • Never let a warm conversation become a fake opportunity — the Gate.
  • Never let one feedback tap rewrite the intelligence score.
  • Never let a user-supplied claim about a contact inflate the score — provenance.
  • Never invent a quote — verbatim or discarded.
  • Never auto-send anything to a contact — the human reviews and sends.
  • Never delete relationship records when moving to Low Priority or Done.
  • Never show Pro features to Starter.
  • Always keep the human as the actor. Observe, inform, support.
