How it works

Three steps. Zero admin.

From the moment you meet someone to the moment you follow up, TAPDconnex sits between you and the cognitive load.

01
Tap
Your NFC card opens your TAPD profile on their phone. They save your contact in one tap. Their details come back to you via WhatsApp.
02
Capture
As you walk away, record the conversation. TAPD transcribes it and structures the conversation through the template you chose. Owner Style applied. Draft ready in seconds.
03
Act
Your Relationship Hub shows who deserves your attention today, why they matter, and exactly what to send. Open the draft, send via WhatsApp, swipe to close the loop.

The principle

We don’t push volume. We help you preserve quality.

TAPDconnex is one slice of your pie. We’re not trying to be your CRM, your calendar, or your inbox. We sit in the small, fragile window between a real-world conversation and the follow-up that actually happens.

The product respects your time. It doesn’t nag. It doesn’t guilt you with vanity metrics. It just makes sure the relationships that mattered stay alive long enough for you to do something about them.

The opportunity is not lost because you failed to network. The opportunity is lost because important details were never captured.
