TAPDconnex

Responsible Recording

Effective date: 4 June 2026

TAPDconnex is designed to help professionals remember business conversations and follow up with context. It is not designed for secret surveillance, unlawful recording, or capturing private conversations without appropriate permission.

Before capturing: Make sure the interaction is appropriate to capture and that you have informed participants or obtained consent where required.

1. Your Responsibility

You are responsible for understanding and following the laws, workplace policies, event rules, and privacy expectations that apply to you and the people you are interacting with.

2. When to Inform Someone

If a conversation may be recorded, transcribed, summarised, or processed by AI, you should inform the other person where required or appropriate. A simple business-friendly line can be used:

“I use TAPDconnex to capture business follow-up notes so I don’t forget important context. Are you comfortable if I capture the key points from our conversation?”

3. What Not to Capture

  • Private, intimate, or highly sensitive conversations.
  • Confidential company information without permission.
  • Information about health, finances, children, legal disputes, or other sensitive categories unless clearly lawful and necessary.
  • Any conversation where the other person objects or appears uncomfortable.

4. Business Purpose Only

TAPDconnex should be used for professional networking, sales follow-up, partnership discussions, recruiting conversations, product discovery, business development, and similar lawful business purposes.

5. AI Processing

Captured information may be processed by AI to create summaries, missing-field prompts, cue sheets, and follow-up suggestions. Review all outputs before sharing or relying on them.

6. If Someone Asks You to Delete Their Information

If someone asks you not to retain or use their information, respect that request where legally required or appropriate. You can delete locally stored captures from your device or request support at tapdconnex@gmail.com.

7. TAPDconnex Is Not Legal Advice

This page is practical guidance only. It is not legal advice. Recording and privacy laws differ by country, state, province, workplace, and event context.
