AI Disclosure

Effective date: 4 June 2026

TAPDconnex uses artificial intelligence ("AI") to assist users with conversation capture, transcription, relationship intelligence, draft generation, conversation summaries, missing-information detection, Conversation Cue Sheets, and follow-up recommendations.

AI is designed to assist users, not replace human judgment. All AI-generated content should be reviewed before being relied upon, shared, or sent.

1. What AI May Do

  • Transcribe captured conversations.
  • Structure conversations into relationship summaries.
  • Identify important themes, signals, and discussion points.
  • Suggest follow-up drafts and communication recommendations.
  • Highlight missing or incomplete information.
  • Generate Conversation Cue Sheets and relationship context.
  • Identify potential relationship signals such as opportunities, connectors, collaborations, or stay-in-touch relationships.
  • Use information provided in a user's Owner Style profile to personalise drafts and recommendations.

2. AI Is Not Perfect

AI outputs may contain errors, omissions, assumptions, bias, hallucinations, or incorrect interpretations. TAPDconnex does not guarantee that AI-generated content is accurate, complete, lawful, appropriate, or suitable for any specific purpose.

AI may misunderstand names, companies, roles, dates, timelines, intentions, priorities, or the context of a conversation.

3. Human Review Required

Users must review and edit AI-generated outputs before sending, relying on, sharing, or acting upon them.

AI-generated messages are drafts only and are never automatically sent on behalf of the user.

Users remain solely responsible for any communication, decision, relationship, transaction, opportunity, or action resulting from the use of AI-generated content.

4. Relationship Intelligence Limitations

Relationship classifications, opportunity signals, collaboration suggestions, connector recommendations, relationship insights, follow-up recommendations, and prioritisation suggestions are generated using AI and should be treated as guidance only.

Users should apply their own judgment when deciding how to engage with, prioritise, follow up with, or evaluate any relationship.

TAPDconnex does not guarantee that any relationship, introduction, opportunity, partnership, collaboration, employment opportunity, speaking engagement, customer lead, investment opportunity, or business outcome will result from the use of the platform.
