Privacy Policy

Effective date: 4 June 2026

TAPDconnex respects privacy and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how TAPDconnex, powered by Iconic Connex, may collect, use, store, disclose, and protect information when users access our website, NFC-enabled experiences, AI-assisted capture tools, and related services.

Important: TAPDconnex is designed for professional and business relationship capture. Users are responsible for ensuring that they have any permissions, notices, or consents required before capturing, recording, transcribing, summarising, or processing conversations involving other people.

1. Information We May Collect

Information provided by users

  • Name, surname, professional title, company, and profile details.
  • Email address, phone number, WhatsApp number, LinkedIn URL, website links, and social profile links.
  • Profile copy, public offering details, portfolio/service information, images, and audio introductions.
  • Messages, support requests, feedback, and information submitted through forms.

Conversation capture information

Where users choose to use capture features, TAPDconnex may process:

  • Voice recordings or temporary audio snippets.
  • Transcripts, summaries, contact notes, relationship context, event context, tags, and follow-up messages.
  • AI-assisted relationship signals, missing-field indicators, and conversation cue information.

Technical and usage information

  • Browser, device, operating system, IP address, diagnostic information, session activity, and usage interactions.
  • Cookies, local storage, or similar technologies required for product functionality, preferences, and account/session continuity.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate TAPDconnex.
  • To display user profiles and NFC-linked pages.
  • To generate AI-assisted transcriptions, summaries, cue sheets, and follow-up suggestions.
  • To support Starter and Pro feature access.
  • To improve usability, reliability, security, and product performance.
  • To respond to support, privacy, or legal requests.
  • To comply with applicable laws and enforce our Terms of Use.

3. AI Processing and Third-Party Providers

TAPDconnex may use third-party providers for hosting, storage, payments, transcription, artificial intelligence processing, analytics, or communication functionality. Information submitted to AI-enabled features may be processed by those providers to deliver the requested functionality.

AI-generated outputs may be incomplete, inaccurate, or misinterpret context. Users must review all outputs before relying on them or sending them to another person.

4. Recording, Consent, and Third-Party Information

Users may capture information about other people during professional conversations. The user who initiates capture is responsible for ensuring that the capture is lawful, appropriate, and permitted. This includes informing participants or obtaining consent where required by applicable law, workplace policy, event rules, or professional obligations.

TAPDconnex does not independently verify whether consent was obtained and is not responsible for a user's unlawful, inappropriate, or unauthorised recording or processing of third-party information.

5. Legal Bases and POPIA

For users in South Africa, TAPDconnex aims to process personal information in alignment with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). Depending on the context, processing may be based on consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests, legal obligations, or another lawful basis recognised by applicable law.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, infrastructure providers, AI/transcription providers, payment providers, professional advisers, authorities where legally required, or parties involved in a business transfer, merger, acquisition, or restructuring.

7. Data Storage and Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain records, comply with laws, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Some MVP functionality may use browser local storage. Users should export important local data before clearing browser data or changing devices.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and TAPDconnex cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. User Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection to processing, withdrawal of consent, or information about how their data is processed. Requests can be sent to tapdconnex@gmail.com.

10. International Users

TAPDconnex may be accessed globally. As we expand internationally, additional privacy rights may apply depending on the user's jurisdiction. Where required, we will apply appropriate safeguards for cross-border processing.

11. Children's Privacy

TAPDconnex is intended for adults and business/professional use. It is not intended for individuals under 18.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of TAPDconnex after changes are published means the updated policy applies.

Questions? Contact tapdconnex@gmail.com.
